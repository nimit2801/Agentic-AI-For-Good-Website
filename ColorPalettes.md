## Recommendation

For the **Claude-inspired aesthetic** with abstract illustrations, I recommend **Option 1: Earth & Stone** or **Option 5: Desert & Clay**.

These palettes provide:
- Warm, approachable backgrounds
- Muted colors that let abstract illustrations shine
- Enough contrast for readability
- Editorial, thoughtful mood
